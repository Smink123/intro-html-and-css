Intro to the best noodle ever

Spicy Tom Yum is a Thai soup which is sour, spicy, and savory. Infused with lemongrass, galangal, and Thai chilies. It's a Thai classic that takes your taste buds on an unforgettable journey.

The history + about Tom Yum!

Spicy Tom Yum, a Thai culinary masterpiece, emerged in the central region of Thailand, reflecting the nation's diverse culinary influences. This iconic dish has evolved from a regional specialty to an international sensation, symbolizing the rich tapestry of Thai flavors and culinary artistry.

Recipe to create Tom Yum Noodles!

Here's a brief and simple recipe for Creamy Tom Yum Noodles:

Ingredients:

  • 200g rice noodles
  • 1 tablespoon red curry paste
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 200g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 200g chicken, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • Fresh cilantro and lime wedges for garnish

Recipe:

  1. Prepare Noodles:Cook rice noodles. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare Curry Base:In a large pot, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add red curry paste and sizzle for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add Coconut Milk:Pour in the coconut milk, stirring to combine with the curry paste.
  4. Add Broth:Add the vegetable or chicken broth to the pot and bring it to a gentle simmer.
  5. Cook Chicken and Shrimp:Add shrimp and chicken to the simmering broth. Cook until the shrimp turns pink and the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Season the Broth:Stir in fish sauce, soy sauce, lime juice, and brown sugar. Adjust the seasoning to your taste.
  7. Mix in NoodlesAdd the cooked rice noodles to the pot, mixing well to combine with the creamy broth.
  8. Serve and Enjoy!Serve into bowls and garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with lime wedges on the side.
